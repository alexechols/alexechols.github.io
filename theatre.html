Theatre

Scotch 'n' Soda Theatre, CMU's only non-major theatre organization has been a big part of my life at Carnegie Mellon. I have done theatre tech for over 10 full-scale productions, largely being involved with theatrical design and construction. While this does provide a fantastic means of pursuing artistic and technical skills, the biggest thing that S'n'S has given me is the ability to effectively work in a team and exercise my professional skills. Budgeting, time management, and team leadership are regular parts of my roles in S'n'S, and as such I have built up a toolbox of skills for large scale projects.

Outside of individual productions, I had the privilege of being the shop manager for Scotch's scene shop, "The Dungeon". Space organization, shop workflow, and proper training protocols are all big interests of mine and my position as shop manager allowed me to flex those skills regularly. Due to knowledge loss from the COVID-19 pandemic, I played an instrumental role in defining the roles and responsibilites of Scotch's shop manager, as well as a number of other positions within the organization.
